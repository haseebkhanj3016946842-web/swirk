MOVIE TOY STORY

A Heartwarming Tale of Friendship and Imagination

Toy Story is a 1995 animated film produced by Pixar and released by Walt Disney Pictures. It was the first feature-length film made entirely with computer animation. The story revolves around a group of toys that come to life when humans aren't around. The main characters, Woody (a cowboy doll) and Buzz Lightyear (a space ranger), start off as rivals but eventually become best friends. The movie explores themes of friendship, jealousy, loyalty, and acceptance in a fun and imaginative way. With its groundbreaking animation and emotional storytelling, Toy Story became a beloved classic for both children and adults.
